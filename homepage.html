Welcome to Lantern – Where Warmth Meets Flavor


At Lantern, we believe a meal is more than just food – it’s an experience. Nestled in a cozy ambiance illuminated by the soft glow of lanterns, we offer a place to unwind, connect, and savor life’s simple joys. Our menu is crafted with love, blending timeless flavors with modern twists to create dishes that light up your palate.

Whether you’re here for an intimate dinner, a celebration with loved ones, or a quiet moment of indulgence, Lantern is your beacon of comfort, taste, and hospitality. Let us brighten your day, one meal at a time.
